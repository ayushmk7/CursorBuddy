CursorBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated April 8, 2026

What this page covers

This policy covers the public CursorBuddy marketing site and waitlist. It does not describe the full product runtime inside VS Code beyond the high-level statements on the site.

Information we collect

If you join the waitlist, we collect the information you submit, which may include your name and email address. We may also receive limited technical metadata needed to operate the site, such as browser type, page requests, security logs, and submission timestamps.

How we use it

We use waitlist information to manage access requests, send launch or preview updates, respond to support inquiries, protect the site, and understand whether the landing page is functioning properly.

How we share it

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us host the site, process waitlist submissions, send email, secure the service, or comply with legal obligations.

Retention

We keep waitlist information for as long as it is reasonably needed to manage preview access, send relevant launch communications, or satisfy legal and operational requirements.

Your choices

You can request that we update or delete your waitlist information by emailing contactayushmadhav@gmail.com. You can also unsubscribe from launch emails using the link in those messages when available.

Children

The site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them through the waitlist.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site or product evolves. If we make material changes, we will update the date on this page and, when appropriate, provide additional notice.
